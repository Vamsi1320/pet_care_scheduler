Pet Wellness Tips

üê∂ Dogs

Young (0-2 years)

  • Start basic obedience training early.
  • Provide age-appropriate toys and plenty of exercise.
  • Feed high-quality puppy food.

Adult (3-6 years)

  • Maintain a consistent exercise schedule.
  • Visit the vet annually for checkups and vaccines.
  • Ensure proper dental hygiene and grooming.

Senior (7+ years)

  • Schedule biannual vet visits for senior care.
  • Provide joint supplements and senior dog food.
  • Watch for signs of arthritis or cognitive decline.

üê± Cats

Young (0-2 years)

  • Provide a scratching post and stimulating toys.
  • Keep the litter box clean and accessible.
  • Ensure kitten vaccinations are up-to-date.

Adult (3-6 years)

  • Feed high-quality cat food with controlled portions.
  • Maintain regular grooming, especially for long-haired breeds.
  • Visit the vet annually for wellness exams.

Senior (7+ years)

  • Provide softer bedding and low-entry litter boxes.
  • Monitor for weight changes or signs of illness.
  • Increase vet visits to twice a year.

üê¶ Birds

Young (0-2 years)

  • Socialize your bird and provide basic training.
  • Ensure a spacious cage with enrichment toys.
  • Feed a diet of pellets, seeds, and fresh fruits/veggies.

Adult (3-6 years)

  • Keep the cage clean and safe.
  • Provide daily out-of-cage time and interaction.
  • Watch for signs of stress or feather plucking.

Senior (7+ years)

  • Regular checkups with an avian vet.
  • Modify perches and cages for easier movement.
  • Provide a calm and quiet environment.
